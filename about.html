Established in 2016, the Indian Institute of Management Jammu is a premier institution dedicated to developing responsible leaders and innovative thinkers. Through academic excellence, impactful research, industry collaboration, and experiential learning, IIM Jammu prepares future-ready professionals who drive organizational success and create a positive impact on society.
